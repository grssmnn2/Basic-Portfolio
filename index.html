About Me

My name is Cecily. I have always been passionate about science and technology, especially involving climate change, animal behavior, and human health. I graduated from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign in 2015 with a Bachelor's in biology, and I am excited to combine my undergraduate degree with my coding skills to become a web developer after graduation from Northwestern Coding Boot Camp.


When I'm not sitting in front of my computer, I like to cook spicy food, binge watch Broadchurch and Issa Rae's show Insecure, and smother my cat Ansel with all the hugs he loves hates.
